Overview

The Lecture Scheduling System is a Flask-based web application that automates the creation of complex academic timetables. Using a deterministic, exhaustive scheduling algorithm, it validates schedules against large datasets to ensure optimal allocation of teachers, subjects, and classrooms without any overlapping conflicts.

  • ‚Ä¢ Distributes sessions evenly: Spreads subject sessions across the week based on sessions-per-week requirements
  • ‚Ä¢ Optimizes classroom usage: Maximizes classroom utilization while preventing double-bookings
